HOW TO BUY

Step 1: Purchase BNB

You can purchase BNB on Binance, Crypto.com or any other exchange.

Step 2a: Send BNB to Trust Wallet

Create a Trust wallet (can be downloaded on app store). Then send your BNB from your exchange of choice to your Trust wallet.

OR

Step 2b: Send BNB to Metamask wallet

Create a Metamask wallet (can be downloaded on app store). Then send your BNB from your exchange of choice to your Metamask wallet.

Step 3: Swap BNB for Smart Chain BNB

This can easily be done when viewing the BNB balance in your Trust wallet/Metamask wallet.

Step 4: Swap $BNB For $WOX on PancakeSwap

Connect your Trust wallet/Metamask Wallet to PancakeSwap, then paste our contract address into the token field to swap $BNB for $WOX.
